Reclamation day

Reclemation day is the very first quest within fallout 76 it requires you to pick up key items like the pipboy a stimpak and a radaway. You also learn how to pick and place your perk cards into your character where after it teaches you how to place your camp and build in it over all it is just a tutorial that introduces you to the game.

This quest happens within vault 76 within the forest region of appalacia this quest rewards the player with a 2 hour no hunger or thirst buff and all the items you have collected on the way out of the vault which includes
    1x 10mm pistol
    200x 10mm rounds
    1x combat knife
    1x light leather armor torso
    consumables
    vault tech supply crate
    scrap
    repair kits
